## Show HN opener

Hi HN, I built Verbum.

It is an open-source TypeScript framework for treating models, terminals, tools, memory, and humans as conversational actors, plus a native Mac app that shows the entire system as a live graph.

The key idea is simple: the right primitive is not "tool calls" or "agent steps". It is messages.

Once everything is a message, observability, replay, search, and human-in-the-loop routing become much simpler.

This launch focuses on local single-machine orchestration first. Collaboration and P2P are intentionally next, not hand-waved.

Would love feedback on the framework shape and the app direction.
